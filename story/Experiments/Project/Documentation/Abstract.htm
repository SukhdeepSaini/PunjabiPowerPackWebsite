Abstract

This project address the health related aspects on which people should focus in order to stay fit and live a health life. Studies show that health related problems are increasing day by day as new diseases are coming in front of the world. The life style of people living across the globe have changed alot in the past few years as well as the eating habits.People now prefer junk food over healthy diet. All these factors are increasing the disease rate and decreasing the health rate for the human beings. This project is an effort to enhance the healthy life style and encourage people to focus more on their health and stay fit. Most essential aspects of health and fitness are covered in the report. Starting from Diet and nutrition to workout schedules. Proper ethnographic research shows that people now days prefer buying products online and they also prefer to get information on specific topics from the web.

Punjabi Power Pack provides platform to get information on all the fitness aspects which are required to stay fit. Buying of bodybuilding supplements , getting information on customized workout routines , reading fitness\health articles and getting information on healthy food. All the points are covered and are properly implemented on the website. I have taken care of all the heuristic Principle to provide user the best experience in performing any task using the website.I believe and hope that this will be a great success.
